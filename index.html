❦ Gifts, Graces & Burdens

Always-on effects auto-apply; conditional ones are switches (they surface in the Exchange when active); narrative ones are reminders. The chevron collapses a card to its name.

★ Reputation & Standing

How you are known in different circles — a name and a value (a number, or a word such as Feared, Honoured, Wanted).

✦ The Sceau du Sort & Fate

Standard operation. Spend 1 Point du Destin at session start; for that session add your bound attribute to one roll per round. The Point is spent permanently.
